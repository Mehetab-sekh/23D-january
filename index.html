Some Quotes of Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose




"One individual may die for an idea, but that idea will, after his death, incarnate itself in a thousand lives."



"It is blood alone that can pay the price of freedom. Give me blood and I will give you freedom"



Netaji played a crucial role in sparking patriotism in hearts of many Indians to gain freedom from British. He was an influential speaker and gave many revolutionary slogans like- “Freedom is not given- it is taken.” and “ We should have but one desire today. The desire to die as India may live.”
